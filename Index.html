About Us

Jeevan foundation is a group of people who believe in a way of life that involves doing good to the society. Living in the world's most polluted capital, we are in strong pursuit of enhancing the flora of our city. We wish to create awareness about global warming, waste management, plantation and its vital role in our lives.

Eastablished in 2018, we have been working with various government and educational bodies and have organised several plantation and awareness drives. We would request everyone to come forward and help us drive our mission, creating a healthier place to live in. We aim to increase plantation and to promote awareness about it among the young generation.
